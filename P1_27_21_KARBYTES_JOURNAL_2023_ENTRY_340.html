Clarification: I did get physically beat up by a few white guys and other categories of human and not just by black guys. Thankfully I have never been seriously physically assaulted. If done right (i.e. having a job which is sufficient to pay for one’s living expenses and some luxuries, living in a decent part of the world which makes living without a car feasible, pursuing an ongoing education, maintaining physical and mental fitness, et cetera), living outside can be more comfortable than living inside (and a way to avoid being encumbered with the hefty costs of renting or owning a housing unit).
