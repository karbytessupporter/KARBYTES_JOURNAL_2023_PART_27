KARBYTES_JOURNAL_2023_ENTRY_326

While I think the outlining accurately depicts a cube comprised of eight equally sized cubes from the vantange of a point in three-dimensional space which is located above that cube and looking down at it, I see that I made some mistakes while initially tracing the outline and the coloring is a bit overdone. Originally I meant to only color in three of the eight cubes completely with solid translucent colors such that the drawing would depict no overlapping cubes which are colored. Perhaps I will try drawing the same object again (from the same vantage) but try to make cleaner outlines and coloring as I described in the previous sentence.
